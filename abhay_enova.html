Online Examination in IMS Engineering College

Online Examination Instructions

(a.) Negative marking: Online examination do not have negative marking but Continues 5 wrong answer will have -5 marks. Each question carry 2 marks.

(b.) During the Exam: Students must complete the 30 multiple choice question exam within the 30 minute timeframe allotted for the exam

(c.) Filling Data: Students must fill all their record (Name, College Name , University Roll No and Date of Birth) after pressing Contiune button at bottom.

(d.) During the Exam: The student may not use their textbook, course notes, or receive help from any source.

(e.) Authority of Invigilator: The invigilator is officially in control of the examination and should be obeyed in all matters relating to the examination.

(f.) Identification: Candidates must produce their identity documents and student identification cards

(g.) Cellular Phones, Pagers, File-O-Fax, and Diaries: Candidates may not take these objects into the examination room.

(h.) Do Not: Candidates do not use Backspace or Enter keys (if used than candidates data will be submitted Automatically).
